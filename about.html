Who We Are

NSU Secure Solutions is a Chennai-headquartered cybersecurity and data privacy firm specializing in both DPDPA compliance advisory and VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing).

Through our flagship platform DPDPA TN, we help businesses in Tamil Nadu and across India achieve robust compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and DPDP Rules, 2025. At the same time, our VAPT services identify and eliminate technical vulnerabilities — ensuring that your data protection strategy is not just legally compliant, but also technically secure.

DPDPA + VAPT = True Security. We combine legal precision with hands-on penetration testing to deliver end-to-end protection — from policy and process design to real-world attack surface hardening.
